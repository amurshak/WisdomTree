Widsom Tree is for seekers of knowledge who wish to be inculcated in the tradition of the great works of human history. All who wander are not lost, but those seeking wisdom often walk a narrow, winding road. The Wisdom Tree hopes to find you at the outset of your journey, to aid in the unceasing quest for self-discovery. Here at Wisdom Tree, we think to understand oneself is to understand the world of which one is a part. The most powerful and enduring books humanity has produced represent the accumulated knowledge of many lifetimes, and a range of perspectives uncapturable for one mind. Given the surfeit of influential, impactful, great works to choose from, starting donw a journey of self-understanding through reading the Great Works can be overwhelming. Wisdom Tree was created to empower you to formulate your own path towards wisdom.
